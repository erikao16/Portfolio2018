Illustration

Below are some of my favorite illustration projects. Some are for clubs, some for class, some independently, and some as part of a team.

Sail Away

This is a poster that I helped design recently. As a graphic designer for Innovative Design, a creative agency at Cal, I worked on a team with three others to complete this project. I created the initial sketch, and created the dragon and fire component in Illustrator.

Nancy Liang study

I did a study of an image I found online, but in the style of an artist that I find inspiration from, Nancy Liang. Particularly, I focused on using texture, abstracting aspects of the image, and maintaining a youthful color palette. Done in Photoshop.

Big Thief

Above is a Facebook cover photo created for a concert that was being held by the club Superb Productions, for which I am an art and design intern. Created in Illustrator, and was later followed by a poster.

Family Equality Poster

As a graphic design intern for the Family Equality Council, I made this poster for an event they were holding. It was an interesting challenge to work in greyscale, but I was able to practice using value effectively!
